What it is

Your orders, always current

Instead of typing every order into a sheet, the dashboard reads the vendor emails you CC and turns them into ready-to-approve order cards. You just review and click Approve. It tracks the parts we need (BOM), what we've ordered, and our vendors — with live coverage and spend.

The honest question

"Why not a spreadsheet?"

A sheet can hold a list — but it can't do the part that saves you time:

  • Vendor emails become approve-with-one-click cards — no manual data entry
  • The data can't get accidentally broken — structured fields, not free-for-all cells
  • It grows — overdue-order nudges, quote parsing, and more, later
How an order shows up

Email → card → approve

Vendor email (CC'd)→ Robot reads & drafts it→ Review Inbox card→ You Approve→ Order tracked

Nothing becomes a real order until a person approves it. Later emails (shipped, tracking, delays) show up as update cards the same way.

Your part — 3 small habits

How to feed it

  • CC purchasing@fourier.earth on vendor threads → the robot sees them
  • Put #amur002 in the email → tags it to this project
  • Put FP# <part number> by each item → auto-matches to our BOM

All optional — it still works without them, they just make it more accurate. For Brex POs, just drag the PDF onto "Add Order" and it fills itself in.

Is it safe?

Locked down & recoverable

  • Only Fourier accounts can open it — outsiders are blocked
  • Automatic backups — anything deleted can be restored (7-day rewind)
  • Every change is logged, and deletes are reversible
  • The AI only ever suggests — a person always approves
